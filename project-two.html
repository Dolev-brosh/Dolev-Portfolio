From Waiting to Engagement

Improving the Loading Experience at monday.com

Company

monday.com

Year

2022

My Role

Design, Resarch

The Problem

Slow dashboard loads at Monday.com frustrated users and generated downtime tickets, affecting ~9% monthly, particularly on large enterprise dashboards.

My Role

I researched user perceptions of wait times, analyzed them through psychological and HCI lenses, and designed a loading experience that reduced frustration and improved perceived efficiency.
